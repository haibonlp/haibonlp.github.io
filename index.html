Haibo Ding

I am a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon AWS AI. I am working on research and production of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing techniques to solve customer problems.

I'm broadly interested in Deep Learning and Natural Langauge Processing. I'm particularly interested in building explainable and trustworthy machine learning models for real-world problems.

Previously, I was a Senior Research Scientist at Bosch Research, where I built machine learning solutions for industrial applications such as dialogue sentiment analysis, customer service, requirement document understanding, knowledge extraction and construction etc. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from University of Utah, where I worked on Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing with a focus on Information Extraction, Affective Event Understanding, and Sentiment Analysis.
