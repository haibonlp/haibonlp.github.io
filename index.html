Haibo Ding

I am a Research Scientist in Bosch Research. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science at University of Utah working with Prof. Ellen Riloff . My research area is Natural Language Processing. My current research focuses on getting computers to learn to understand human language with minimal supervision. I have also worked on Information Extraction, Affective Event Understanding, and Sentiment Analysis.
